Project Intermediate Goods

Data Mining Project

For this project we were asked to make a short/medium term prediction of some kind of value, the value chosen was intermediate goods. This prediction was made through a database extracted from the internet.

After cleaning the database of irrelevant or empty values, we proceeded to a study of the database where we made an analysis of its normality, seasonality and typical statistical values. After this, we make numerous correlograms and ARMA models until we find the right one. Once the right model is obtained, we make the prediction of future values, the distribution of the errors and, based on the data obtained, we make a conclusion.
